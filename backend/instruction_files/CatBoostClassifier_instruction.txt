MODEL_SELECTION_REASONING:
CatBoostClassifier should be prioritized for classification tasks with 2–3 feature subsets, especially on balanced datasets with numerical or high-signal categorical features. It excels in biomedical and structured tabular domains where features carry strong discriminative power. Encoding support makes it well-suited for datasets with mixed feature types.

MODEL_AVOIDANCE_REASONING:
Avoid CatBoostClassifier for single-feature subsets or datasets dominated by weak, noisy, or low-information features (e.g., elemental composition fields like Fe, Si, Ca). Performance also degrades noticeably on imbalanced datasets and purely categorical datasets without strong class-separating features.
