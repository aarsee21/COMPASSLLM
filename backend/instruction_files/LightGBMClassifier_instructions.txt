MODEL_SELECTION_REASONING:
  LightGBMClassifier should be prioritised for tabular classification tasks with
  mixed or numerical feature spaces, especially when datasets are relatively balanced
  and contain a small number of highly discriminative features. It is particularly
  effective on biomedical and structured numerical datasets where gradient boosting
  can exploit feature interactions without requiring extensive preprocessing.

MODEL_AVOIDANCE_REASONING:
  Avoid LightGBMClassifier as the sole model for severely imbalanced datasets without
  applying class weighting or resampling strategies, as accuracy variance is high in
  such conditions. Also reconsider when inference latency is critical in production
  environments, as ensemble tree models carry higher prediction overhead than linear
  or distance-based models.
