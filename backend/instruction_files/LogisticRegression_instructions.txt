MODEL_SELECTION_REASONING:
LogisticRegression should be prioritized for binary and multi-class classification on balanced, numerical-dominant datasets where linear decision boundaries are plausible. Best suited for 2–3 feature subsets with scaled inputs and encoding enabled. Performs reliably on structured biomedical and tabular data with low dimensionality and high-signal features.

MODEL_AVOIDANCE_REASONING:
Avoid LogisticRegression for single weak or uninformative features, highly imbalanced datasets without compensation, and non-linear classification problems. Performance also drops on purely categorical feature spaces without strong class-separating signals, and when encoding is not applied.
