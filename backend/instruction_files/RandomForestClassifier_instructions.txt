MODEL_SELECTION_REASONING:
RandomForestClassifier should be prioritized for classification tasks with 2–3 feature subsets on balanced, numerical-dominant datasets where non-linear feature interactions exist. Its ensemble nature makes it robust across biomedical, clinical, and structured tabular domains. Encoding support makes it effective on mixed feature type datasets as well.

MODEL_AVOIDANCE_REASONING:
Avoid RandomForestClassifier for single weak or uninformative features, highly imbalanced datasets without compensation, and purely categorical feature spaces with low class separability. It also adds computational overhead compared to simpler models when the dataset is small and linear.
